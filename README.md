### 🏆 Featured Research & Development
#### 🧤 Neural Voice: Sign-Language-to-Voice Translator
*A collaborative flagship project developed with [Shajidur Rahman](https://github.com/Shajidur-Rahman)*

> This project utilizes an **ESP32-S3** and a suite of **Flex Sensors** to bridge the communication gap for the speech-impaired. By mapping complex hand gestures to digital signals, we provide a real-time voice for those who communicate through signs.
> 
> **Key Tech:** `C++`, `ESP-NOW Protocol`, `IMU Data Fusion (MPU6050)`, `Signal Processing`.
